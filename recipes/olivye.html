Olivier Salad

Description

Olivier salad is a traditional salad dish in Russian cuisine made with finely diced ingredients all mixed up with mayonnaise.
There are many different variations to this salad, but the main ingredients include potatoes, vegetables, eggs, bologna and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 6-7 potatoes
  • 5 eggs
  • 3 pickled cucumbers
  • 200g of canned green peas
  • 500g of bologna
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 250g of mayonnaise or to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare a large bowl beforehand for combining all the diced ingredients.
  2. Wash and peel potatoes. Boil in salty water until done. Take potatoes out of the water and cool them down. Once cooled, dice them into small cubes.
  3. Boil eggs for 10 minutes to get yolks completely done. Cool eggs down, shell and dice them.
  4. Dice bologna and pickled cucumbers the same way.
  5. Open canned green peas and drain them with a sieve. Add them to the bowl.
  6. Season the salad with some salt and black ground pepper. Dress your salad with some mayonnaise and mix it.
  7. The salad is ready. Refrigerate and serve!
