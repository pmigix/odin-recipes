Beef Stir-Fry

Description

A quick and easy meal packed with vegetables, beef and mouth-watering flavours.

Ingredients

  • 200g of white rice
  • 400ml of water
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 450g of beef sirloin
  • 250g of broccoli
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce

Steps

  1. Rinse rice under cold water for 30 seconds using a sieve, then combine rice and 400ml of water into a saucepan.
    Put on the lid and turn the heat to medium-high and bring rice to the boil.
    Once rice comes to a boil, set a timer for 10 minutes and reduce the heat to low.
    Turn off the heat and let rice steam while preparing the beef stir-fry.
  2. Cut beef sirloin into small pieces, broccoli into florets, bell pepper into thin slices, carrots into thin rounds and chop onion.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet, lightly season the beef with salt and black ground pepper, cook and stir until browned.
  4. Move beef to the side and add broccoli, bell pepper, carrots and onion to the center. Cook and stir vegetables for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir everthing together and season with soy sauce.
  6. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables are tender.
  7. Serve and enjoy the meal!
